# 2.6.4 — 2026-06-05

## Removed

- **Local SQLite study mirror** (`CT_MIRROR_*` config) removed. The mirror served search and counts from a local FTS5 index that cannot reproduce ClinicalTrials.gov's server-side field-scoped search and MeSH expansion — condition counts diverged 4–5× from live, `fields` selection was silently dropped, pagination was broken (no `nextPageToken`), and the metadata-only schema could not return `resultsSection` at all. All search, count, and record lookups now always use the live API. Resolves [#65](https://github.com/cyanheads/clinicaltrialsgov-mcp-server/issues/65), [#66](https://github.com/cyanheads/clinicaltrialsgov-mcp-server/issues/66), [#68](https://github.com/cyanheads/clinicaltrialsgov-mcp-server/issues/68), [#69](https://github.com/cyanheads/clinicaltrialsgov-mcp-server/issues/69).

## Fixed

- **`clinicaltrials_find_eligible`** `recruitingOnly: true` no longer includes `NOT_YET_RECRUITING` studies — it now filters to `RECRUITING` only. Resolves [#67](https://github.com/cyanheads/clinicaltrialsgov-mcp-server/issues/67).
